Issue #001
 
 
When it comes to books about personal finance or investing, my interest has generally trended downward (pun intended!). I’m mainly drawn to those stories about a company’s founders, its CEOs, its employees – in other words, the books which have a pretty significant crossover with my other favorite nonfiction categories like bios and science & tech. But as a new MBA candidate, I’ve been starting to get my hands dirty in the deeper worlds of finance, and had fun checking out Motley Fool podcaster Alison Southwick’s book recommendations – and I hope you will too! Also, don’t miss my picks for the best weird business exposés, and more below.
Audible Editor Laura
