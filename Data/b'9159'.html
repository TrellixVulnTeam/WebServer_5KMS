Day 1: Super motivated, super organized, super on top of everything.

If this sounds like a familiar story to you, then check out this week's video. In it, we'll be digging into that all-important question...

"How can I stay on top of my homework and studying - over the entire school year?"

In my experience, achieving this comes down to doing three things consistently:
  1. Keeping a well-maintained calendar and to-do list
  2. Breaking assignments down into manageable chunks
  3. Working every day so you don't get rusty (even if it's just a little bit)
