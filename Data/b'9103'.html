Have Confidence in Your Hiring!
Effective recruitment is one of the skills that sets a great manager apart from a poor one. Hiring the right person first time can save your organization time and money.

So, you need to be confident that the person you hire is the right fit for the role and the team. You can establish this using the questioning techniques in our article, How to Run Competency Based Interviews. Also, watch our video, 6 Traits of a True Professional, to discover what to look for in competent people.

There are plenty of pitfalls to avoid when hiring, and you can identify some of them with our article, 10 Recruitment Mistakes.
